A Travel-Inspired Brand

Travii — your chocolatii budii — is a chocolate brand inspired by the spirit of travel and the natural beauty of Hawaii. Designed for travelers, the pocket-sized packaging offers a convenient way to enjoy a delicious bite of chocolate while exploring the scenic landscapes of Hawaii. The Travii Milk Yogurt series takes a different direction, drawing inspiration from the De Stijl movement — a Dutch art movement originating in 1917 that emphasized geometric forms, primary colors, and a pursuit of universal harmony and order.
