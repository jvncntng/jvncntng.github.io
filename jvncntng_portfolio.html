About
JVNCNTNG

I am a Visual Arts graduate and multidisciplinary designer with 4+ years of experience in graphic design and social media strategy. Currently serving as the Graphic Designer for the Culinary and Retail Division at the Polynesian Cultural Center.

Originally from the Philippines, I refined my skills at BYU-Hawaii, developing a strong foundation in Visual Fine Arts and Web Frontend Development. I love solving problems through design—whether that's creating a new brand identity or coding a responsive website.
