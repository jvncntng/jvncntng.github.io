Print Meets Movement

A collection of poster designs and motion graphics projects spanning print and digital media. The '1984' poster reimagines George Orwell's dystopian novel through bold typography and restrained composition — using negative space and a dominant diagonal to create a sense of unease. The Jeepney Sinage project celebrates Filipino street culture and visual identity. In motion, 'Twilight Wishes: From Sunset to Starlight' is an animated piece exploring the transition from day to night, while the 'Joyland' title sequence brings a cinematic, editorial quality to motion design.
