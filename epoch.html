The New Age of Artificial Intelligence

Epoch AI Magazine is a conceptual editorial project inspired by the rapid rise of artificial intelligence and its profound implications for society. The design explores the tension between the clinical precision of AI systems and the deeply human questions they provoke — authorship, consciousness, creativity, and control. The visual language is deliberately austere: heavy typography, geometric forms, and restrained color to mirror the cold logic of machine intelligence while leaving space for human unease.
