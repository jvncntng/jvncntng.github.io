Overview

The Work

The Polynesian Cultural Center — Hawaii's #1 attraction — draws over 700,000 visitors annually to its 42-acre living museum in Laie, O'ahu. As in-house Graphic Designer for the Culinary and Retail Division, I create the visual identity that guests encounter across dining, retail and wayfinding touchpoints throughout the property.


The scope spans brand identity and logo design for named culinary concepts, through to menus, A-frame signs, food signage and large-format environmental signage. Every piece has to work in a high-traffic, high-visibility environment — clear, on-brand, and worthy of one of Hawaii's most photographed destinations.
