About the Work

The 2024 BYUH Undergraduate Research Conference brought together students from across campus to showcase academic work spanning research papers, poster sessions, and creative projects in visual arts, music and writing. The conference was held on April 3–4, 2024 at BYU–Hawaii, Laie.


As part of the Intern Team alongside Chutamas Tavivongpaiboon and Brigham Gomez Perez, I served as Graphic Designer and Media Marketing Intern — handling the full scope of visual communication for the conference. My most significant contribution was producing and editing the conference proposal video, filming the committee's Vice President introducing the event's theme, which was used to pitch and gain approval for the conference.


Beyond the proposal video, I managed event photography across conference sessions, designed promotional graphics and posters shared via the BYUH Library's Instagram, and assisted in updating and managing the urc.byuh.edu website throughout the conference cycle.
